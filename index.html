Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College was established in 1996 by Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Trust under the stewardship of Dr M.Mohan Babu, a film artist and former member of Rajya Sabha.

Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College is one of the largest institutions in Andhra Pradesh and one-of-its-kind in Rayalaseema offering BTech, MTech, Diploma, MCA and MBA programmes. The college is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur (JNTUA), Andhra Pradesh.

Dr. M. Mohan Babu’s keen interest towards learning and academics comes from his father, Narayana Swamy Naidu, a teacher and headmaster in a school in Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh. Dr. M. Mohan Babu set up SVET as a hub of knowledge and academic excellence. People of all nations, races, beliefs, social strata or academic background are welcome. What once started off as an institution with 216 students, it has risen to be an institution with close to 10000 students, 5 colleges and two schools. The trust’s institutions have a cumulative full-time faculty of over 350, initially starting off as 10. SVET is the largest private institution in the Rayalaseema region and operates in collaboration with the Governments of India and Andhra Pradesh.
